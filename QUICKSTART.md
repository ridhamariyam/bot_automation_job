# JobRocket - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to **JobRocket**! 🚀 Automatically apply to jobs on LinkedIn without lifting a finger.

## What is JobRocket?

JobRocket is a smart job application bot that:
- 🤖 Automatically applies to jobs matching your criteria on LinkedIn
- 📧 Falls back to Gmail if a job requires email submission
- 📊 Tracks all applications in your dashboard
- ⚡ Runs 24/7 once started

## Getting Started (2 minutes)

### Step 1: Register
1. Go to the **Register** page
2. Enter your name, email, and a secure password
3. Click "Sign up"

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile Setup
You'll be guided through 4 simple steps:

**Step 1 - Profile Info**
- Your name, phone, and email
- Target job titles (e.g., "Full Stack Developer, Backend Engineer")
- Target locations (e.g., "London, UK" or "Remote")
- Key skills relevant to your field

**Step 2 - Connect LinkedIn**
- Enter your LinkedIn email and password
- We'll verify your credentials (encrypted and secure)
- The bot will use this to auto-apply to jobs

**Step 3 - Upload CV (Optional)**
- If you have a CV, upload it for better application accuracy
- You can skip this for now

**Step 4 - Review & Start**
- Review your settings
- Click "Start Applying 🚀"

### Step 3: Start the Bot
1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. You'll see a "Start Applying 🚀" button
3. Click it to start
4. Watch as the bot applies to jobs!

## Dashboard Overview

### Bot Status
Shows whether the bot is running or stopped.
- **Green dot + Running** = Bot is actively applying
- **Gray dot + Stopped** = Bot is not active

### Quick Stats
- **Total Applications** = All-time applications
- **Applied Today** = Applications submitted today
- **Viewed** = Jobs that viewed your profile

### Recent Applications
See all jobs the bot has applied to, including:
- Job title and company
- Location
- Application status
- When it was applied

## Features

### Auto-Apply to LinkedIn Jobs
- Matches your criteria perfectly
- Applies to "Easy Apply" positions
- Respects LinkedIn's application limits

### Email Fallback
- If a job requires email application, the bot uses your configured email
- Sends a professional application letter
- No manual action needed!

### Live Tracking
- Dashboard updates every 10 seconds
- See real-time application results
- Track interviews and responses

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific with Titles**
   - Instead of "Engineer", try "Full Stack Developer" or "Backend Engineer"
   - More specific titles get better matches

2. **Set Realistic Locations**
   - Use multiple locations if willing to relocate
   - Mark "Remote" if you prefer work-from-home

3. **Update Your CV**
   - Even a simple CV helps with better application matching
   - Ensures forms are filled out accurately

4. **Check Your Email**
   - Some jobs use email applications
   - Configure your Gmail in settings for this

5. **Let It Run**
   - The bot respects LinkedIn's rate limits
   - Don't stop/start constantly - let it work!

## Security & Privacy

- Your LinkedIn credentials are **encrypted** on our servers
- We **never** share or sell your data
- Only used for job applications
- You can change credentials anytime in settings

## Troubleshooting

### Bot Won't Start
- ❌ You might not have completed profile setup
- ✅ Solution: Go to Settings → Update your LinkedIn credentials

### No Applications Yet
- ❌ The bot might still be searching
- ✅ Give it a few minutes and refresh
- ✅ Check that your target titles are specified

### Gmail/Email Issues
- ❌ Some jobs require email application
- ✅ Configure your Gmail in Settings
- ✅ Ensure 2FA is not blocking SMTP access

## Need Help?

1. Check your dashboard status
2. Verify your LinkedIn credentials in onboarding
3. Ensure you have target titles and locations set
4. Contact support if issues persist
